About Me

Hi, I’m Daniel Owolabi, a UI/UX designer and web developer based in Lagos, Nigeria. With over three years of experience in the industry, I specialize in crafting intuitive, user-focused designs that solve real-world problems and deliver seamless digital experiences.

My journey began with a passion for blending creativity and technology. I’ve worked on diverse projects, from designing mobile apps for startups to developing responsive websites for established brands. My expertise spans UI/UX design, web development, data analysis, graphic design, video editing, and Python programming.

I believe in a user-centric approach, where every design decision is informed by research and empathy for the end user. Whether it’s prototyping in Figma, coding in JavaScript, or analyzing data to drive insights, I’m committed to delivering solutions that are both beautiful and functional.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring new design trends, contributing to open-source projects, and sharing insights through my blog. I’m always excited to collaborate on innovative projects, so feel free to reach out and let’s bring your vision to life!
